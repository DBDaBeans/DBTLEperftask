Bacoor National
High School-Main
An educational institution formerly known as Bacoor Municipal High School
(Padre Mariano Gomez National High School) estabished in 1972 whose buildings
grew in number and population of students grew larger. Bacoor National High School
and its annexes transformed itself into a recognized school in the town of Bacoor.
It still continues to operate as of today.
